About Me

I am studying Computer Science at Lassonde School of Engineering at York University. As a passionate independent developer, I also work as the game programmer for rbkn Studio. Alongside my journey in Computer Science, I often participate in game jams hosted by ITCH.IO using Unity as the game engine. Considering how video games have been my favorite leisure for so long, I have find myself fascinated by the mechanics involved in one. Hence, as a creative industry that brings me joy, I aspire to pursue a career in video game development.

Nearing the end of my 3rd year in Computer Science, I have found Artificial Intelligence to be my favorite subject, perhaps due to an inspiration from Alan Turing's works.

Besides honing my skills in game development and catching up to studies, I often try to learn digital art in Krita, which comes from an appreciation for the amazing art styles starting from 8-bit Pixel Art to the realistic AAA art I have seen in the gaming industry. My DeviantArt profile displays the rookie artworks I have done so far.
