About Me

I graduated from BSc (Hons.) Computer Science at Lassonde School of Engineering at York University in Spring 2023. I currently work as the lead game programmer for Studio Cloud Grey. Alongside my journey in Computer Science, I often participate in game jams hosted by ITCH.IO using Unity as the game engine. Considering how video games have been my favorite leisure for so long, I have find myself fascinated by the mechanics involved in one. Hence, as a creative industry that brings me joy, I aspire to pursue a career in video game development.

Having studied Computer Science, I have found the crooks and riddles of computer security to be my favorite area, perhaps due to an inspiration from the god of cryptography and Artificial Intelligence Alan Turing's works.

Besides honing my skills in game development and catching up to studies, I often try to learn digital art in Krita, which comes from an appreciation for the amazing art styles starting from 8-bit Pixel Art to the realistic AAA art I have seen in the gaming industry. My DeviantArt profile displays the rookie artworks I have done so far.
